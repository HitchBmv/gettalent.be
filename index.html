Executive Search & Recruitment

We specialize in identifying, attracting, screening, and recruiting Directors, Managers, and Experts with proven track records for international corporations and Belgian companies.

Insight
Our objective is to understand your organization, including its structure, market position, culture, and required competencies. An initial intake with Business and HR partners is essential to start our collaboration.

Search Strategy
After understanding your vacancy, Get Talent will identify target companies to track similar talents. We utilize our network and conduct targeted research through various innovative methods beyond traditional job boards to find the best candidates.

We believe that creativity, dynamism, personal commitment, and professionalism are vital in driving a successful executive search process. Our focus is on open communication, responsiveness, and results.

Candidate Follow-Up in Your Recruitment Process

Expertise in Offer Negotiations

Ongoing Candidate Identification

Tests & Assessments

Get Talent provides a comprehensive assessment for every executive search mission, ensuring a thorough evaluation of the final candidate selected.

Partnership with Assessment Experts
We collaborate with leading assessment professionals to deliver an all-encompassing service. Our approach includes behavior-oriented exercises and advanced assessment techniques that evaluate a broad spectrum of competencies. This partnership enhances our ability to provide objective and standardized evaluations, ensuring consistency throughout the process.

Innovative Assessment Methods
Our assessments include personality tests that candidates can complete online from their homes, allowing us to gain insights into their thinking and behavior across various scenarios. The personality assessment consists of 90 questions, which candidates can complete in approximately 30 minutes. This tool explores 20 different personality facets, such as leadership, creativity, and autonomy, and is available in 12 languages to meet diverse needs.

Multilingual Support and Rigorous Evaluation
Our multilingual team conducts systematic interviews in French, Dutch, and English, ensuring effective communication with candidates. In addition to oral assessments, we offer written language proficiency tests in multiple languages, including French, Dutch, English, German, and Spanish. These tests are designed to measure various aspects of language skills within a 30-minute timeframe. By leveraging these robust assessment strategies, Get Talent is committed to identifying the right leaders for your organization while providing transparent communication and ongoing support throughout the recruitment process.
